Demystifying the SMM Ecosystem

Social Media Marketing (SMM) panels operate in a vast, multi-million dollar grey-area industry. They power the follower counts of influencers, fuel the engagement metrics of marketing agencies, and jumpstart communities for modern brands. Yet, reliable, non-commercial information about them is notoriously difficult to find.

We built SMM Panel Info to fill that void. By breaking down the complex supply chains—from primary providers to API-connected child panels—we aim to educate users, protect buyers from prevalent scams, and provide transparent insights into the mechanics of artificial social growth.
