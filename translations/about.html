About This Project

Demystifying the Industry

SMM Panel Info was created to serve as a clear, unbiased, and ad-free educational resource detailing the complex mechanics of the Social Media Marketing (SMM) panel ecosystem.

The SMM industry operates largely in the shadows. It is an unregulated, highly lucrative digital grey market where information asymmetry heavily favors the sellers. We realized there was no centralized repository of truth explaining how API reselling actually works, how to detect prevalent hit-and-run scams, or how to identify genuine primary providers.

Our Independence

We are strictly a non-profit, independent informational platform. We do not sell SMM services, we do not operate a panel, and we do not accept financial compensation, sponsorships, or "free balance" from any panel in exchange for favorable placement in our market research.

Our market assessments and panel mentions are derived strictly from analyzing public sentiment, monitoring tracking directories, and leveraging deep industry experience to separate legitimate operators from fraudulent storefronts.
